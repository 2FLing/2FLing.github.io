Learn About Ecommerce

Purpose of scoping projects

ECommerce sites tend to have specific needs such as shipping,payment,and taxes that you will need to flush out before building. When you know how to scope a project,and you do it before you start allocating resources, you can prevent cost overages and you can prevent performance issues.

Listen and repeat

Listen to the client and paraphrase what they say, and say it right back to them. The reason why it is so important is because clients use ambigugous words and phrases. Miscommunications are costly and can harm your reputation, so make sure that you are listening and repeating.

Clarify jargon

Scale is kind of a buzzword in the eCommerce world Asking clarifying questions builds trust with your clients. Three things that should always clarify: Buzzwords, like scale, and anything visual, and anything interactive.

Critical constraints

Projects usually have three elements:Fast, Cheap and good. All three are interrelated.You can optimize one or two, but not all three. If you want to design something quickly and to a high standard, but then it will not be cheap. Because you will need more developer and designer. If you want to design something quickly and cheaply then it won`t be of high quality. If you want to design something affordable and high quality, but it will take a much longer time. Ask the client to pick which two elements they want.

Project Strategies

Project Strategies
Waterfall:This is for agencies and clients that to do this kind of work often Multiphase:That is for the client who doesn`t have the experience or they don't have enough money. Minimum Viable Product(MVP):That is for people who have a little experience and little budget to work with.
Overall, if you have the time and budget, then go for waterfall. If you don`t have the budget or if you are trying to do a lot of custom work, try MVP. If you doing something in the middle, go with the multiphase approach.
